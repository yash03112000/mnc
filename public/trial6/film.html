ABOUT US

We at Film Club, IIT Kanpur provide a boat to the islands of this art. We tunnel the club’s functioning and activities through various events. Cinematography, Screenwriting, Casting, Shooting, Sound recording, Production, and Editing are the aspects which are thoroughly studied during the club’s overall walkthrough. Film Club takes interest in a variety of work ranging from short films, skits, sketches to documentaries. We implement a whole bunch of activities around the years including Orientation videos, Freshers’ Attempt, Batch Video, Summer workshop and a lot more.
We also take part in competitions all over India through various Fests and Events and try to learn from the attributes lying within the tasks.

We aim to foster creative Inculcation and a better understanding and depth in the world of filmmaking. The goal is to create quality content for youtube and other social media platforms so as to enter the realm of professional Film making. Keeping the hotshot works apart, the primary aim for us as a club is to perforate into the hearts of ingenious minds that can carry on the torch of glory riding up on the path laid by Film Club through the years.
