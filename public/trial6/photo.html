ABOUT US

“In photography there is a reality so subtle that it becomes more real than reality.” — Alfred Stieglitz

We are a crew of photography enthusiasts whose mission is to preach, practice, and master photography skills for the shared benefit of the self and campus community. We have our own studio where a lot of breathtaking photos are clicked. We hold interactive lecture series and workshops for the beginners followed by fun photo-walks in Kanpur, Varanasi, Nainital, Rishikesh, and Mumbai in the last couple of years.

An annual exhibition is put up during Antaragni showcasing the ‘best of the best’ photos clicked by the campus community throughout the year. It has always been one of the major attractions in the events ground. Some of the very renowned photographers ( like the greatest street artist Vineet Vohra, the FIFA 2018 photographer Lopamdra Talukdar, and the world record holder Shobhit Tiwari ) had visited us to share their experiences and teach us some of the latest techniques in the game.

OUR EVENTS

  1. Exhibition and competition during orientation
    An exhibition displaying the work of the club to motivate the incoming students to explore photography and an open competition for them to have some fun!
  2. Coverage of in-campus events
    Campus is always filled with exciting events happening every now and then, and the photography club tries to provide glimpses into these events to the campus junta through social media.
  3. Workshops
    Conducts various workshops by professionals that help students explore and develop their skills and interest in the field of photography.
  1. Inter IIT Cultural Meet
    Participate in the events of photography in the cultural meet.
  2. Antaragni Exhibition
    An exhibition which provides a platform for campus members to display their best clicks.
  3. Galaxy
    The club hosts a series of competitions in various genres of photography among different pools during Galaxy.
  4. Summer Workshop
    A 3-week workshop that helps familiarize the basics of photography through a series of lectures, photowalks, and lectures by professional photographers.
